Timothy James

  • Unemployed Student
  • Shark Tank Aficionado

Tim and Nicole met one month before Tim was moving 1856 miles to Boulder, CO. It was love at first sight for Tim who made his mother wait hours in the driveway of a friend's graduation party so he could work up the courage to ask for Nicole's phone number.

Over the next four years Tim took countless cross-country flights to never miss a Valentine's Day in Connecticut & always successfully surprise Nicole for her sorority formals. Tim even went so far as to swap his phone with his mother's so she could send text messages pretending to be Tim to keep Nicole from suspecting anything while he was actually flying across the country!

They’ve survived living thousands of miles apart for four years and hundreds of miles apart for two more while Nicole finished graduate school. After living 4.2 miles apart for a few more years, they finally decided to move in together & the rest was history!
